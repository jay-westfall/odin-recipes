Broccoli Casserole


Description

Are you looking for another side dish for your Thanksgiving meal? Look know further than the broccoli casserole.

This delicious and easy Broccoli Casserole recipe uses fresh broccoli florets (or frozen) tossed in a creamy Velveeta sauce, with Ritz crackers baked on top. It's warm and comforting and an easy side dish that pairs well with anything!

Ingredients

  • 32 ounces broccoli
  • 16 ounces Velveeta
  • 1 sleeve Ritz Crackers crushed
  • 2 tablespoons of melted salted butter
  • 1 cup grated cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp pepper
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder
