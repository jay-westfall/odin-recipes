Broccoli Casserole


Description

Are you looking for another side dish for your Thanksgiving meal? Look know further than the broccoli casserole.

This delicious and easy Broccoli Casserole recipe uses fresh broccoli florets (or frozen) tossed in a creamy Velveeta sauce, with Ritz crackers baked on top. It's warm and comforting and an easy side dish that pairs well with anything!

Ingredients

  • 32 ounces broccoli
  • 16 ounces Velveeta
  • 1 sleeve Ritz Crackers crushed
  • 2 tablespoons of melted salted butter
  • 1 cup grated cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp pepper
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees. Prepare a 9X13 baking dish by spraying the bottom and sides with non-stick spray. Set aside.
  2. Boil the broccoli florets in salted water for 3 minutes, drain them, and put them into a large mixing bowl.
  3. Season the broccoli with salt, pepper, garlic and onion powder. Stir the Velveeta Cheese into the broccoli until fully incorporated and all the cheese is melted.
  4. Pour the broccoli cheese mixture into the casserole dish and distribute evenly. Sprinkle the shredded cheddar evenly on top of the broccoli.
  5. Mix the crushed ritz crackers with the melted butter then sprinkle on the top of the casserole. Bake for 25-30 minutes until the cheeses are bubbly. Remove from oven and serve immediately.
